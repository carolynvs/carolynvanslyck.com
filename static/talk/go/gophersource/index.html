GopherSource is an initiative to strengthen and diversify the Go ecosystem through building up contributors to upstream Go and key Go projects from the within the community.

Let's create inclusive communities where people feel safe joining in and have a clearly-defined path towards becoming members and leaders

* landing page * debunking OSS hype * support contributors and maintainers
